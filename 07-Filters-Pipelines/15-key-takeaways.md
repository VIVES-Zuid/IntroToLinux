# Key Takeaways


- Filters are the building blocks of powerful text processing
- Pipes enable complex data transformations through simple tools
- grep is essential for pattern matching and searching
- Regular expressions multiply the power of text tools
- Practice building pipelines step by step
- Each tool has a specific purpose - choose the right tool for the job
